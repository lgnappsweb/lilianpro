# **App Name**: GlamGestão

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Analytics: Provide a comprehensive overview of financial summaries, most-sold products, and recent orders to keep the administrator informed.
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM): Enable full management of customer profiles including personal details, purchase history, and direct WhatsApp contact for personalized service.
- Product Catalog Management: Maintain a detailed product catalog with names, brands, categories, pricing, codes, descriptions, and photo uploads for easy inventory tracking.
- Sales Transaction System: Facilitate efficient recording of sales, product selection, diverse payment methods (cash, Pix, card, credit), discounts, and tracking of payment statuses (paid, pending, overdue).
- Financial Overview & Smart Alerts: Offer a consolidated financial view of total receipts, outstanding balances, and overdue payments, alongside automated notifications for impending and overdue customer payments.
- Secure Administrator Authentication: Implement restricted access to the application via email and password authentication, leveraging Firebase Authentication for security.
- AI Sales Assistant Tool: Utilize an AI tool to generate intelligent product recommendations for individual customers based on their purchasing history, and suggest optimal times for follow-up communications, enhancing sales strategy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Brand Color: A rich, elegant dark pink (#C2185B), embodying sophistication and the core identity of the beauty business. This hue anchors interactive elements and brand accents.
- Background Color: A subtle, desaturated warm pink (#F7F3F5) serves as a soft and inviting canvas, harmonizing with the primary color while maintaining a clean, spacious feel, blending into the modern aesthetic with shades of #FFFFFF and #F5F5F5.
- Accent Color: A vibrant magenta-fuchsia (#D028A9), chosen for its dynamic contrast, draws attention to critical calls-to-action and adds a modern, energetic flair to key highlights.
- Both headlines and body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clear, and objective appearance, ensuring readability and a professional look across all informational and transactional screens.
- Clean and elegant line-art icons that complement the modern and minimalistic design. Icons will be visually subtle yet convey functionality clearly.
- A structured and responsive layout featuring professional dashboards with 'card' based information displays, emphasizing clarity and efficient data consumption. Rounded buttons and minimal design elements contribute to a modern, organized aesthetic adaptable to various screen sizes.
- Subtle and smooth transitions for state changes and navigation, providing a fluid and sophisticated user experience without distractions.
